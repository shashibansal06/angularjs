How it works

Business

If you own a small business, you can signup and submit your software projects for free. DevGap will help you by suggesting software projects templates and reviewing your requirements; your project will be then matched to developer profiles, based on their skills. DevGap will assist you until the the project is ready to start and even help you with fees and contract negotiations!

Developer

If you are a software developer, you can sign up for free and update your profile with your coding skills; DevGap will match your profile to existing and new projects, based on your skills and your weekly availability. DevGap will assist you for free until the project starts and even help you with fees and contract negotiations!
